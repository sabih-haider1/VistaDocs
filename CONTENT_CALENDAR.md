## Keyword Research Notes

**How to Find Keywords:**

1. **Google Search Console:**
   - Go to Performance → Queries
   - Find queries with high impressions, low CTR
   - Write targeted content for those queries

2. **Competitor Analysis:**
   - Search your target keywords
   - Analyze top 3 results
   - Identify gaps (what they missed)

3. **Answer the Public:**
   - Free tool for question-based queries
   - Enter "UAE visa" or "Next.js"
   - See what people are asking

4. **Reddit/Forums:**
   - r/dubai for visa questions
   - r/nextjs for technical questions
   - Find recurring problems with no good answers

## Internal Linking Strategy

**Rule:** Every blog post must link to 1-2 service pages

**Examples:**

- Technical posts → link to "Web Development" or "Custom Software Development"
- Regulatory posts → link to specific visa services (Employment Visa, Golden Visa)
- Case studies → link to services used (Process Automation, Business Setup)

**How to Link:**
- Natural anchor text: "employment visa processing" not "click here"
- Link in first 200 words when contextually relevant
- Maximum 3 internal links per post

## Quality Checklist (Before Publishing)

Every post must have:

- [ ] Specific, actionable takeaways (not generic advice)
- [ ] Real examples (code, data, client stories)
- [ ] Visual elements (screenshots, diagrams, tables)
- [ ] Scannable structure (headings, lists, bold)
- [ ] Internal links to 1-2 services
- [ ] Author bio with credentials
- [ ] Meta title ≤60 chars
- [ ] Meta description 150-155 chars
- [ ] Primary keyword in H1, 1-2 H2s

## Stop Doing These (Common Mistakes)

❌ **Generic tutorials** ("What is Next.js?")  
✅ **Specific implementations** ("How to implement ISR with MongoDB")

❌ **Keyword-stuffed** ("UAE visa services in Dubai UAE for visa processing")  
✅ **Natural language** ("How to process an employment visa in UAE")

❌ **AI-generated fluff** ("In today's fast-paced world...")  
✅ **Direct answers** ("The employment visa process takes 14-20 days. Here's why...")

❌ **Copying competitors** (same info, different words)  
✅ **Original insights** (your experience, data, mistakes)

---

**Remember:** Quality > Quantity. 12 excellent posts beat 50 mediocre ones.

**Focus:** Write the definitive guide that others will cite and link to.

**Test:** Can only you write this? If yes, publish. If anyone could write it, rewrite.
