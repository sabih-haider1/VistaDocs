### 3. Golden Visa (5 & 10 Year Residence)

**Service Type**: Core Visa Service  
**Processing Time**: 14-30 working days  
**Service Location**: Throughout UAE

#### What is Golden Visa
Long-term residence visa (5 or 10 years) with automatic renewal offering:
- No sponsor required after initial approval
- Family sponsorship included
- Longer validity than standard residence visa
- Extended stay outside UAE without visa cancellation (up to 6 months)

#### Eligibility Categories

**Investors**
- Property investment: AED 2 million minimum
- Business investment: AED 2 million minimum in UAE company
- Investment fund deposits: AED 2 million minimum

**Entrepreneurs**
- Tech startup founders with government approval
- Innovative project owners

**Skilled Professionals**
- Doctors, scientists, engineers in specialized fields
- PhD holders
- Outstanding students

**Service Type**: Core Visa Service  
**Processing Time**: 5-10 working days  
**Service Location**: Throughout UAE

#### What You Get
- Residence visa renewal (2-3 year validity)

### 10. Medical Fitness & Emirates ID Processing

**Service Type**: Mandatory Processing  
**Processing Time**: 
- Medical: 24-48 hours for results
- Emirates ID: 7-10 working days  
**Service Location**: Throughout UAE

#### Medical Fitness Testing
Mandatory health screening at DHA/MOHAP approved centers including:
- Chest X-ray (tuberculosis screening)
- Blood test (HIV, Hepatitis B & C, VDRL)
- General physical examination
- Pregnancy test (for females of certain age groups)

#### Emirates ID Processing
Government-issued identity card processing including:
- Biometric enrollment (fingerprints, photo, signature)
- Application submission to ICP
- Card production and delivery
- Renewal services

#### Approved Medical Centers
- Al Tadawi Medical Center
- Aster Clinic (multiple locations)
- Prime Medical Center
- NMC Medical Center
- Emirates Hospital
